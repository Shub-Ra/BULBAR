Our Commitment to Sustainability

At BULBAR, we believe fashion should be bold and kind to the planet. Our oversized and bulky styles are crafted using eco-friendly materials and ethical production methods.

  • 100% organic cotton and recycled fabrics
  • Reduced water and energy usage in manufacturing
  • Fair wages and safe working conditions for workers
  • Packaging made from recycled and biodegradable materials

About BULBAR

BULBAR was born from the desire to merge bold oversized fashion with sustainability. We believe that fashion can be both statement-making and responsible. Our mission is to create unique, bulky pieces that not only turn heads but also tread lightly on the planet.

Every item is thoughtfully designed using eco-friendly materials and ethical practices, ensuring you look good and feel good.
